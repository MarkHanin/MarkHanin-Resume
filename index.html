My name is Mark Hanin and I am currently an undergraduate student at the University of California, Riverside (UCR). I am interested in health law, public health advocacy, and engaging communities to understand how healthcare policies can sometimes place people at a disadvantage.

As an undergraduate, I have been actively working to making sure that I raise awareness of the importance of health and health resources as part of my campaign of promoting health literacy among community members. I have have taken constructive steps to achieve this goal through the creation of my own webinar series as well as through my involvement with some of the Pipeline Programs at UCR's School of Medicine.

Aside from my work towards promoting health literacy among local communities, I am a part of many communities on my college campus. I am a part of the University Honors Program at my university, where I serve as a University Honors Ambassador. I am also a part of my student government, where I am the Vice-Chair of the Student Voice Committee, the only non-political committee within our university's Student Government.
